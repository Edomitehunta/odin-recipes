Braised Lamb Shanks

Lamb shanks braised in red wine with fresh rosemary, garlic, and tomatoes.Excellent served with polentas or roasted garlic mashed potatoes my family's favourite, to soak up the wonferful sauce. This is a fantastic dish to make for company, as all the prep work is done at the beginning. All you have to do is wait!

Ingredients

  • 6 Lamb Shanks
  • Salt and pepper
  • 3 large carrots, cut into round shapes
  • 2 onions, chopped
  • 10 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (750) bottle red wine
  • 1 (28 ounce) can whole peeled tomatoes with juice
  • 1 (10.5) can condensed chicked broth
  • 1 (10.5 ounce) can beef broth
  • 5 teaspoons chopped fresh rosemary
  • 2 teaspoons chopped fresh

Steps

  1. Cook the lamb shanks in oil until they are brown on all sides
  2. Add carrots onions, and garlic to the pot and saute. Stir in the remaining ingredients.
  3. Return the shanks to the pot bring to a boil, cover, and simmer until the meat is tender.
  4. Uncover the pot and simmer for about 20 minutes, then remove the shanks.
  5. Boil the sauce until thick, then spoon it over the lamb shanks to serve.
